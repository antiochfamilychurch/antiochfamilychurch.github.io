Mission

Our mission is to be a community of faith where Lives are Changed by God's Grace. We choose to live this out, as we learn how to Love God and to Love Our Neighbors.

We have a mission to reach people with the message of HOPE. Hope in this life, as well as hope for eternity. Jesus offers everyone forgiveness and a right relationship with God. We are all learning together what that means, and how we can best live this out.

Our goal is nothing less than to Change the World, one life at a time, by loving God, and loving people.
